Here you will find my research centered on the **systematics**, **phylogeny**, and
**taxonomy** of **Neotropical and Andean rove beetles** (Coleoptera: Staphylinidae).
Each paper below represents a step forward in **understanding** the entomological
**diversity** of **South America** and how life **evolves** from the lowlands to the high
Andes.
