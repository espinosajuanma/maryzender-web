#### Introduction

We report for the **first time** the **complete immature stages** of the
Neotropical riodinid butterfly ***Leucochimona hyphea*** (Cramer, 1776).

#### Material & Methods

Based on individuals **reared** in Madre de Dios, Peru.

#### Results

We illustrate **all five larval instars**, in addition to the pupa and the egg,
and provide the duration of each stage. We also provide **evidence for the
presence of ant organs** on A8 of the larvae and pupa of ***L. hyphea***, and
report its **natural host plant** at the study site, ***Spermacoce latifolia***
Aubl. (Rubioideae: Rubiaceae), which represents a **new host record** for this
species.
